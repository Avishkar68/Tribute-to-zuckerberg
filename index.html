BORN AT 14 MAY 1984

Mark Zuckerberg, the enigmatic visionary, embodies the perfect blend of intellect, ambition, and innovation. With his unparalleled drive, he has not only disrupted the tech industry but also transformed the very fabric of human connection. His audacious journey, from a Harvard dorm room to the helm of a social media empire, is a testament to his unwavering determination and ability to challenge the status quo.
